Bolognese

Introduction

It doesn’t take a lot of ingredients (or a lot of money) to make a classic Bolognese recipe.

What it does take, though, is patience for the sauce to achieve the ideal authentic texture. You’re going to go low and slow—it’ll take around 3 hours, but most of that cook time is hands-off.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
